Green Space Management System

As part of a team project in my first year of university, I contributed to the design and implementation of a software solution that supports the operations of an organization responsible for planning, maintaining, and managing urban green spaces:

◦ Core features included the registration and management of collaborators (with associated jobs and skills), dynamic team formation, vehicle and equipment inventory tracking with maintenance scheduling, and a full agenda system for assigning, scheduling, and monitoring field tasks.

◦ The system was developed in Java using Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) following TDD and was supported by a GUI developed with JavaFX.
